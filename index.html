Privacy Policy — Lumora

Last updated: 17 June 2026

Lumora (“the app”) is a calming one-line puzzle game published by the Lumora developer. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle. The short version: Lumora does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information we collect

None. Lumora does not ask you to create an account, does not request your name, email, contacts, location, photos, or any other personal information, and contains no third‑party advertising or analytics SDKs.

Data stored on your device

To make the game work, the app saves a small amount of information locally on your device only (using your browser/WebView localStorage). This never leaves your phone and is not transmitted to us or anyone else. It includes:

  • Your level progress and current journey.
  • Whether you have unlocked “Lumora Complete”.
  • Your settings (theme, sound, haptics, reduce‑motion, warm‑light).
  • Your daily streak and, if you use the Breath Trainer, your recorded breathing rhythm.

You can erase all of this at any time by clearing the app’s storage/cache or by uninstalling the app.

Purchases

Lumora offers one optional one‑time in‑app purchase (“Lumora Complete”). Purchases are handled entirely by Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment‑card details. Google’s handling of that transaction is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

Permissions

The app may use your device’s vibration (haptics) to provide gentle feedback while you play. This is purely a local hardware feature and collects no data. You can turn haptics off in Settings.

Children’s privacy

Lumora is suitable for all ages and collects no personal data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a new “Last updated” date.
